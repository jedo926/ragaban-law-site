About RN & Co

RN & Co is a law firm providing legal advisory and consultation services to corporate clients and institutions.

The firm represents its clients in court and before judicial authorities and advises on complex legal matters requiring structured legal analysis and compliance with applicable laws, court procedures, and regulatory requirements. RN & Co supports clients in managing legal risk, structuring legal positions, and resolving disputes, whether through advisory work or court proceedings.
